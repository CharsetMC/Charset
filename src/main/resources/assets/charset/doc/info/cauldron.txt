\title{The Cauldron}

The cauldron has received major upgrades!

\- It is now a fully qualified fluid container! It can store up to one bucket of any fluid in the game.
\- Putting lava into a cauldron makes anything dropped into it burn.
\- You can fill buckets by dropping them into the cauldron!
\- By adding dye to water, you can create Dyed Water. It can be used to dye items! You can also clear the color with a Sponge.
